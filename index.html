How I work

A process built for clarity and momentum

Each project is different — but every good outcome follows a thoughtful sequence.

01 — Discover

Understand the problem space

Stakeholder interviews, user research, competitive analysis. Align on the real problem before defining any solution.

02 — Define

Frame the right problem

Synthesise research into clear insights. Journey maps, problem statements, and success criteria that ground all subsequent decisions.

03 — Design

Explore and converge

Rapid ideation, wireframing, and prototyping. Multiple directions tested quickly before committing to a direction.

04 — Deliver

Ship with intent

High-fidelity designs, annotated specs, and hands-on engineering involvement to ensure the build matches the vision.

05 — Measure

Learn and iterate

Post-launch analytics, usability follow-ups, and iteration cycles. The work doesn't end at ship — it deepens.
