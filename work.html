Doctoral Researcher · HCI · Digital Health

Social Buddy - Virtual Agents for Isolated Adolescents

Doctoral research project involved designing, developing, and evaluating an AI-driven Virtual Reality environment featuring embodied conversational agents to support medically isolated adolescents. The system aimed to safely mediate and improve social interactions between hospitalised teenagers and their peers. The work spanned the entire HCI lifecycle, including participatory co-design workshops, technical VR development in Unity, and rigorous mixed-methods statistical evaluation to measure the impact of the virtual mediators.
